About Me

I am a student artist studying art and design at
the University of Illinois at Chicago, class of 2021.
A lot of my work started when I learned photography
and from there I started to branch out into other forms
of art, as well as design.

Text Editing Project

This is a screenshot of the text I edited for my digital header.
The project was challenging as I tried to manipulate the font to
match the complexity of the curves and sharpness of my header image.
I decided that a serif font would be the best choice as I can round
the serifs at the ends of the font to be round and sharp, as
well as stretching the lettering to match the line work within
the image.

Roommates: A Twine Game

Roommates is a twine game I created. In this game, you are an
entity and you have to find something to do. In the images I have
screenshots of the actual game. Originally the game was supposed
to be a horror game, but my love for comedy and irony led me to
take the game on a different route. The first two images are of
the outline of the game and the stylesheet I used to edit the
text and background of each scene. The last two images are parts
of the game when you pick your choices.
